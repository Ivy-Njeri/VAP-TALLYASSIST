⬇️ How to Download, Install & Activate TallyPrime

1. Download TallyPrime

Visit the official Tally website: tallysolutions.com/download
Click on “Download” – the setup file will begin downloading.

2. Install TallyPrime

Once downloaded, open the .exe file.
Click “Install” and follow the prompts. You can choose the destination folder or use the default.
Wait for installation to complete.

3. Activate Your License

After installation, launch TallyPrime.
On the startup screen, choose:

  • Activate a New License – if you have a new serial number
  • Reactivate Existing License – if you’ve used it before
  • Use Educational Mode – for free training use
Enter your Serial Number, Activation Key, and your admin email to complete activation.

4. You're Ready!

Once activated, TallyPrime opens to the Gateway screen. You can now start creating your company and working with ledgers, vouchers, and more.
