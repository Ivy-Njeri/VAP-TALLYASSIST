Topic 1 :📘 What Are Financial Reports?

Financial reports are official records that summarize the financial performance, position, and cash flow of a business. In Tally, these reports are generated automatically based on the transactions entered.

🎯 Why Are Financial Reports Important?

  • Monitor business performance and profitability
  • Track assets, liabilities, income, and expenses
  • Support decision-making and forecasting
  • Ensure tax, audit, and compliance readiness
  • Assist in securing loans or attracting investors

📑 Key Financial Reports in Tally

1. 📈 Profit & Loss Account

Shows income and expenses over a period to calculate Net Profit or Loss.
Path: Gateway of Tally → Display → Profit & Loss A/c

2. 📉 Balance Sheet

Displays company’s financial position: Assets vs. Liabilities + Equity.
Path: Gateway of Tally → Balance Sheet

3. 💰 Cash Flow Statement

Tracks cash inflows and outflows during a specific period.
Path: Gateway of Tally → Display → Cash Flow

4. 🔁 Trial Balance

Lists debit and credit balances of all ledgers. Helps detect imbalance/errors.
Path: Gateway of Tally → Display → Trial Balance

5. 📋 Day Book

A detailed log of all vouchers entered for any day or period.
Path: Gateway of Tally → Display → Day Book

6. 📄 Ledger Reports

Shows all transactions under a specific ledger (like Cash or Bank).
Path: Gateway of Tally → Display → Account Books → Ledger

7. 📚 Group Summary

Summarizes balances for a group of ledgers (e.g., Sundry Debtors or Sales).
Path: Gateway of Tally → Display → Account Books → Group Summary

8. 📌 Ratio Analysis

Analyzes key financial ratios like Current Ratio, Debt-Equity, etc.
Path: Gateway of Tally → Ratio Analysis

📤 Exporting and Printing Reports

  • Press Alt + E to export reports to Excel, PDF, HTML
  • Press Alt + P to print reports directly
  • Use configuration options to include/exclude columns

📌 Tips for Using Reports in Tally

  • Always set the correct date range (F2 key)
  • Drill down reports using Enter to view vouchers
  • Use configuration (F12) to customize the display
  • Regularly verify ledger balances with source documents
  • Back up your company before audits or report sharing

✅ Summary

Tally offers a comprehensive set of financial reports that are essential for running a business efficiently. With minimal data entry, you can get real-time insight into the company’s performance and be fully compliant for tax and audits.
