A Bank Reconciliation Statement (BRS) is a report that compares the bank balance in your books with the balance in your actual bank statement. It helps identify:

  • Unpresented cheques
  • Bank charges and interest not recorded
  • Customer deposits directly to bank
  • Errors in data entry

  • Ensures your books match your actual bank balance
