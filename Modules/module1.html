Topic 3:Company Creation in Tally

Company creation in Tally is the first and most important step that allows you to begin maintaining accounts, inventory, and taxation for a business. It defines your financial year, bookkeeping start date, and all company details.

ğŸ“ Steps to Create a Company

  • Open TallyPrime or Tally ERP 9.
  • From Gateway of Tally, press Alt + F3.
  • Select Create Company.
  • Fill in Company Name, Address, Country, State, Financial Year, Currency.
  • Press Ctrl + A to save.
  • Press F11 to enable Accounting, Inventory, and Tax features.
